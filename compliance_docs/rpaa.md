# RPAA - Retail Payment Activities Act

## Core Requirements

- **Registration**: Payment service providers performing retail payment activities must register with the Bank of Canada.
- **Operational Risk Management**: Establish, implement, and maintain a robust risk management and incident response framework.
- **Incident Reporting**: Notify the Bank of Canada and affected parties **without delay** of any incident with material impact on end-users, other PSPs, or clearing houses.
- **Safeguarding End-User Funds**: Hold funds in trust or use equivalent protection measures (e.g., insurance/guarantee).
- **Significant Change Notification**: Notify the Bank before making significant changes that could impact operational risks or fund safeguarding.
- **Consumer Protection**: Implement proper complaint handling mechanisms.
- **Governance & Oversight**: Strong internal controls and accountability for retail payment activities.

**Purpose**: To protect end-users, reduce operational risks, and promote confidence in retail payment systems in Canada.
