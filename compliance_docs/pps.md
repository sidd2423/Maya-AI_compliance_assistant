# PPS - Risk Management Standards for Prominent Payment Systems (Bank of Canada)

## Core Risk Management Standards

### 1. Legal Basis
- Must have a well-founded, clear, transparent, and enforceable legal basis in all relevant jurisdictions.

### 2. Governance
- Clear and transparent governance arrangements.
- Board oversight with appropriate skills and conflict management.
- Strong risk management framework with clear accountability.

### 3. Comprehensive Risk Management
- Sound framework to manage legal, credit, liquidity, operational, and other risks.

### 4. Credit Risk
- Effectively measure, monitor, and manage credit exposures.
- Maintain sufficient financial resources to cover largest potential losses.

### 5. Liquidity Risk
- Maintain sufficient liquid resources to meet obligations under stress scenarios.
- Robust stress testing and contingency plans.

### 6. Settlement Finality & Money Settlements
- Provide clear final settlement (preferably same-day or intraday).
- Prefer central bank money for settlements when practical.

### Other Important Areas
- Operational Risk Management
- Collateral Management (low risk assets, conservative haircuts)
- Participant Default Rules
- Transparency and Disclosure

**Purpose**: To ensure Prominent Payment Systems in Canada maintain high standards of safety, efficiency, and resilience.
